WALLETIFAI MONEY SAVING APP

Walletifai intelligently detects bills and subscriptions and helps manage them on a daily basis.

Role

Research
Ideation/wireframing
User Testing
Prototyping
Hi-fidelity design
Developer handoff

Goal

Develop a money saving app to help people get a better view of their financial life by showing them the most useful insights generated by the AI algorhythm. Helping manage bills and subscriptions with insight into the past and projecting into the future.

Approach

1. Defining MVP features by conducting user research and analysing competitor products.
2. Identifying current user behaviour and pain points, creating user personas. Ideating solutions.
3. Wireframes best ideas, testing and iterating.
4. Creating high fidelity prototypes, testing, iterating and handing design to the developers.

PROCESS

I collaborated with company founders, developers and growth/marketing team to design, test and iterate.
