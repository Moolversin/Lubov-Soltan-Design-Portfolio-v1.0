REAL ESTATE SEARCH APP

Book a viewing, collect and share you favourite listings.

Gaida pivoted to a different product since this design was created.

INTRO

Role

UX/UI Designer

Tools

Sketch

Goal

Develop a bare minimum version of previously designed app.

Approach

  1. Re-evaluate MVP features according to user testing;
  2. Define red routes;
  3. Wireframe;
  4. Create high fidelity prototypes, test.

Team

PROCESS

Red routes and analysis

First wireframes

Red routes

I fleshed out the most frequently used flows as red routes. These are the next iteration of the wireframes sketches.

Booking a tour

Main goal of any user who uses this app is to find a property by connecting with an agent and booking a tour.

Sharing a listing

Most users have a family member or partner who they share a decision to buy or rent a property with.

Add to favourites

Save to compare later or share later with other people.

Final flows

Booking a tour

  • Search location;
  • Show properties on the map/as a list;
  • View listing;
  • Request a tour;
  • Success/error message;
  • Send confirmation.

Share a listing

  • Search location;
  • Show properties on the map/as a list;
  • View listing;
  • Share;
  • Select recipient;
  • Success/error message.

Save to favourites

  • Search location;
  • Show properties on the map/as a list;
  • View listing;
  • Save;
  • Success/error message;
  • View in favourites/back to listing.
