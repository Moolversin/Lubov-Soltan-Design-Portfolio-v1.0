Lubov Soltan is a Graphic/Visual Designer and Illustrator based in Toronto.

Came from Belarus, lived in the Netherlands and Canada.

Posesses Masters Degree in Fine Arts from Yanka Kupala State University of Grodno, Belarus. Worked as as a Graphic Designer and Illustrator since 2010. Focused on bringing strong design solutions for web, mobile and print, developing Brand Identities for international tech conferences, individuals and companies.

Considers empathy, consistency, communication and discipline key qualities of an effective and creative designer. Constantly learning, making real impact for tech communities.
