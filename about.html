Lubov Soltan is a Graphic/Visual Designer and Illustrator based in Toronto.

Grew up and received Fine Arts Degree in Grodno, Belarus. Lived in the Netherlands for four years and now in Canada sinse 2018.

Started Graphic Designer, Illustrator and Artist career in 2010. Focused on bringing strong design solutions for web, mobile and print, developing Brand Identities for international tech conferences, individuals and companies.

Considers empathy, consistency, communication and discipline key qualities of an effective and creative designer. Constantly learning, making real impact for tech communities.
