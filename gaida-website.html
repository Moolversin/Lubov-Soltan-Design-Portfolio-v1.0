GAIDA.AI WEBSITE

Project Description & Goals

My goal was to produce website layout and illustrations for pages with different type of content. This project interested me because I had an opportunity to develop the brand image further by creating illustrations that express what GAIDA is about.

What is GAIDA?

Gaida.ai aims to make process of finding and buying a home seamless and efficient for both agents and buyers. Gaida is a bulgarian bagpipe. The word sounds like guide in English and is also a backronym: GAIDA = Guide in AI and Data Analytics.
